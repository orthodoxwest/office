[hymn]
# SOURCE: A Noted Hymnary for the Anglican Breviary and Other Prayer Books, p. 13
Rector potens, verax Deus

O God of truth, O Lord of might,
Who orderest time and change aright,
And sendest the early morning ray,
And lightest the glow of perfect day;

Extinguish thou each sinful fire,
And banish every ill desire:
And while thou keepest the body whole,
Shed forth thy peace upon the soul.

O Father that we ask be done
Through Jesus Christ, thine only Son,
Who, with the Holy Ghost and thee,
Shall live and reign eternally. Amen.

blessing rest upon all the work of our hands, that being cheered with
