[opening-rubric]
The following is said only when beginning a session of the Office.

[open-my-mouth]
Open my mouth, O Lord, to bless thy holy Name: cleanse my heart from all vain,
evil, and wandering thoughts; enlighten my understanding and kindle my affections,
that I may fittingly recite this Office with attention and devotion, and so may
be meet to be heard before the presence of thy divine Majesty. Through Christ
our Lord.
R. Amen.

O Lord, in union with that divine intention wherewith thou thyself didst
offer thy praises to God while upon the earth, I now recite this Office to thee.


[sacrosanctae]
To the holy and undivided Trinity, to the crucified humanity of our Lord
Jesus Christ, to the fruitful virginity of the blessed and ever Virgin Mary,
and to the whole company of the saints, be everlasting praise, honour, power,
and glory from every creature, and unto us the remission of all our sins for ever,
world without end.
R. Amen.

V. Blessed is the womb of the Virgin Mary, which bore the Son of the Father eternal.
R. And blessed are the paps which gave suck to Christ the Lord.

[closing-rubric]
Then is said secretly Our Father and Hail Mary.
