[hymn]
Rerum Deus tenax vigor

O GOD, creation's secret force,
Thyself unmoved, all motion's source,
Who from the morn till evening's ray
Through all its changes guidest the day:

Grant us, when this short life is past,
The glorious evening that shall last;
That, by a holy death attained,
Eternal glory may be gained.

O Father, that we ask be done
Through Jesus Christ, thine only Son,
Who, with the Holy Ghost and thee,
Shall live and reign eternally. Amen.

of thy Son upon the Cross: grant, we beseech thee, that we, by the memory of
his most holy Passion, may ever have strength to resist the assaults of sin.
Through our Lord Jesus Christ thy Son, who with thee and the Holy Spirit
liveth and reigneth, God, for ever and ever.
