[psalm-antiphon-1]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 688 (Common of Holy Women not Virgins, Paschaltide)
While the King * sitteth at his table, my spikenard sendeth forth a sweet-smelling savour, alleluia.

[psalm-antiphon-2]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 689 (Common of Holy Women not Virgins, Paschaltide)
We will run after thee * because of the savour of thine ointments: exceedingly do the virgins love thee, alleluia.

[psalm-antiphon-3]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 690 (Common of Holy Women not Virgins, Paschaltide)
Lo, the winter is past, * the rain is over and gone: rise up, my love, and come away, alleluia.

[psalm-antiphon-4]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 690 (Common of Holy Women not Virgins, Paschaltide)
Come, O thou my chosen one, * and I will set my throne within thee, alleluia.

[psalm-antiphon-5]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 691 (Common of Holy Women not Virgins, Paschaltide)
This woman * is beautiful among the daughters of Jerusalem, alleluia.

# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Vespers book, p. 674 (Common of Holy Women not Virgins, Paschaltide)
