# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 653 (Common of a Confessor and Bishop, Paschaltide)

# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 653 (Common of a Confessor and Bishop, Paschaltide)
The Lord, * therefore, assured him by an oath that he would bless the nations in his seed, alleluia.

# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 654 (Common of a Confessor and Bishop, Paschaltide)

# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 656 (Common of a Confessor and Bishop, Paschaltide)
