# Eastertide seasonal overrides (Easter Sunday through the Saturday after Pentecost)
# Alleluia is doubled throughout Eastertide.

[alleluia]
Alleluia, alleluia, alleluia.

[short-responsory-compline]

[versicle-lauds]
V. Let my mouth be filled with thy praise, alleluia.
R. That I may sing of thy glory and honour all the day long, alleluia.

[versicle-vespers]
V. Let my prayer be set forth in thy sight as the incense, alleluia.
R. And let the lifting up of my hands be an evening sacrifice, alleluia.
