[hymn-lauds]
# SOURCE: office-hymns.zip (All Hail! Ye Infant Martyr Flowers, Holy Innocents Vespers.docx); draft archdiocesan Lauds/Vespers books, pp. 98, 85
Salvete, flores Martyrum

All hail! ye infant martyr flowers,
Cut off in life's first dawning hours:
As rose-buds snapt in tempest strife
When Herod sought your Saviour's life.

You, tender flock of Christ, we sing,
First victims slain for Christ your King:
Beneath the Altar's heavenly ray
With Martyr palms and crowns ye play.

All honour, laud, and glory be,
O Jesu, Virgin-born, to thee;
All glory, as is ever meet,
To Father and to Paraclete.
Amen.
