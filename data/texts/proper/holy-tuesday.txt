Behold, O Lord, and see, * for I am in trouble; hear me speedily.

[psalm-antiphon-2]
Plead my cause, * O Lord; deliver me from the unjust and deceitful man.

[psalm-antiphon-3]
I cried by reason of mine affliction unto the Lord, * and He heard me out of the belly of hell.

[psalm-antiphon-4]
O Lord, I am oppressed, * undertake Thou for me; for I know not what to say unto mine enemies.

[psalm-antiphon-5]
The ungodly said Let us oppress the righteous man, * because he is clean contrary to our doings.

[benedictus-antiphon]

V. Christ became obedient for us unto death.
R. Yea, the death of the Cross.

[collect]
O Almighty and everlasting God, give us grace so to use the solemn and mysterious memorial of the Lord's Suffering, that the same may be unto us a mean whereby worthily to win thy forgiveness.
