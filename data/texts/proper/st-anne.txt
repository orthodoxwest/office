[benedictus-antiphon]
Give her of the fruit of her hands, * and let her own works praise her in the gates.

[magnificat-antiphon]
She is beautiful * among the daughters of Jerusalem.

[commemoration-antiphon]
While the King sitteth at his table, * my spikenard sendeth forth the smell thereof.

[commemoration-versicle]
V. Many daughters have done virtuously.
R. But thou excellest them all.

[collect]
O God, Who in thy kindness gave blessed Anne the grace to be the mother of her who mothered thy only-begotten Son, graciously grant that we who keep her feast may be helped by her intercession with thee.
