# SOURCE: Antiphons on the Magnificat for Saturday Vespers Throughout the Year.docx, p. 10 (Qui cælorum)
Thou that upholdest * the throne of the heavens and beholdest the depths, O Lord, King of kings; that weighest the mountains and holdest the earth in the hollow of thine hand: give ear, O Lord unto us, in the midst of our groanings.
