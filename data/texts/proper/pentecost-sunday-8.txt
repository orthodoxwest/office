# SOURCE: Antiphons on the Benedictus for Sunday Lauds Throughout the Year.docx, p. 5 (Ait dominus villico)
The lord said * unto the steward, How is it that I hear this of thee? Give an account of thy stewardship, alleluia.

[magnificat-antiphon]
# SOURCE: divinum-officium Tempora/Pent08-0.txt [Ant 3] — check against diurnal
What shall I do? * For my lord taketh away from me the stewardship. I cannot dig, to beg I am ashamed. I am resolved what to do, that, when I am put out of the stewardship, they may receive me into their houses.
