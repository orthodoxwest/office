[benedictus-antiphon]
When ye fast, * be not, as the hypocrites, of a sad countenance.

[magnificat-antiphon]
Lay up for yourselves treasures in heaven, * where neither moth nor rust doth corrupt.

[commemoration-antiphon]
Turn ye even to me * with all your heart, and with fasting, and with weeping, and with mourning, saith the Lord.

[commemoration-versicle]
V. O Lord, have mercy upon us.
R. For we have sinned against thee.

[collect]
Grant, O Lord, to thy faithful people, that they may undertake with fitting piety the venerable solemnities of fasting, and complete them with steadfast devotion.
