# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Vespers book, p. 201 (Friday within the Octave of Corpus Christi)
May the children * of the Church be like the olive branches, round about the table of the Lord.

# SOURCE: draft Temporal Cycle Vespers book, p. 203 (I Vespers of Corpus Christi)
O how sweet * is thy Spirit, O Lord, who that thou mightest shew thy kindness unto thy children, givest them that sweetest bread from heaven, fillest the hungry with good things, and sendest the rich and scornful empty away.
