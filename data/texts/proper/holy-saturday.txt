[psalm-antiphon-1]
O death, I will be thy death; * O grave, I will be thy destruction.

[psalm-antiphon-2]
They shall mourn for Him * as one mourneth for his only son, for the innocent Lord hath been put to death.

[psalm-antiphon-3]
O all ye nations, behold * and see my sorrow.

[psalm-antiphon-4]
O Lord, deliver my soul * from the gates of the grave.

[psalm-antiphon-5]
O all ye that pass by * behold, and see if there be any sorrow like unto my sorrow.

[benedictus-antiphon]
There were women sitting over against the sepulchre * weeping, and making lamentation for the Lord.

[magnificat-antiphon]
O death, I will be thy death; * O grave, I will be thy destruction.

[commemoration-antiphon]
O all ye that pass by * behold, and see if there be any sorrow like unto my sorrow.

[commemoration-versicle]
V. My flesh shall rest in hope.
R. Neither wilt Thou suffer thine Holy One to see corruption.

[collect]
Grant, we beseech thee, O Lord, that as we who are redeemed by the precious Blood of thy Son, so also cleansed by his holy Passion, we may this night pass from death to life.

[nunc-dimittis-antiphon]
Now Thou dost dismiss Thy servant, O Lord, * according to Thy word in peace, alleluia.
