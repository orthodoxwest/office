[hymn-lauds]
# SOURCE: divinum-officium SanctiM/08-01 [Hymnus Laudes] — check against diurnal
Beate Pastor, Petre, clemens accipe

Peter, blest shepherd, hearken to our cry,
And with a word unloose our guilty chain;
Thou! who hast power to open the gates on high
To men below, and power to shut them fast again.

Praise, blessing, majesty, through endless days,
Be to the Trinity immortal given;
Who in pure unity profoundly sways
Eternally alike all things in earth and heaven.
Amen.

[hymn-vespers]
# SOURCE: divinum-officium Sancti/08-01 [Hymnus Vespera] — check against diurnal
Miris modis repente liber, ferrea

In wondrous mode set free, lo, at the Lord's command
The galling iron chain doth fall from Peter's hand,
From Peter, shepherd blest, who doth with gentle sway,
His faithful children lead in virtue's fragrant way,
And e'er with watchful love the tempter drive away.

Now to the Father be eternal glory done;
Our songs we raise to thee, O everlasting Son;
O Spirit from on high, thy throne we bow before;
To thee be honour, praise, and glory evermore:
The Holy Trinity we worship and adore.
Amen.
