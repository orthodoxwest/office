[benedictus-antiphon]
# SOURCE: Antiphons on the Benedictus for Sunday Lauds Throughout the Year.docx, p. 4 (Attendite a falsis)
Beware * of false prophets, which come to you in sheep’s clothing, but inwardly they are ravening wolves. Ye shall know them by their fruits, alleluia.

[magnificat-antiphon]
# SOURCE: divinum-officium Tempora/Pent07-0.txt [Ant 3] — check against diurnal
A good tree cannot bring forth evil fruit, * neither can a corrupt tree bring forth good fruit. Every tree that bringeth not forth good fruit, is hewn down and cast into the fire. Alleluia.

[magnificat-antiphon-first]
# SOURCE: Antiphons on the Magnificat for Saturday Vespers Throughout the Year.docx, p. 3 (Unxerunt Salomonem)
Zadok the priest * and Nathan the prophet anointed Solomon king in Gihon: and the people came up rejoicing and said, Let the king live for ever.
