# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Vespers book (Common of Feasts of the B.V.M.; Sancta Maria, I Vespers)
O holy Mary, * help thou the suffering, strengthen the faint-hearted, comfort the sorrowful; pray for the people, entreat for the clergy, intercede for all womankind vowed unto God: may all acknowledge the help of thy prayer, who celebrate thy holy festival.
