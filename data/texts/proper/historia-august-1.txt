# SOURCE: Antiphons on the Magnificat for Saturday Vespers Throughout the Year.docx, p. 4 (Sapientia ædificavit)
Wisdom hath builded her house, * she hath hewn out her seven pillars: she hath subdued the nations, and in her own might hath she trodden under the necks of the proud and lofty.
