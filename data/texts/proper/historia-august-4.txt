# SOURCE: Antiphons on the Magnificat for Saturday Vespers Throughout the Year.docx, p. 5 (Sapientia clamitat)
Wisdom crieth * aloud in the broad places: Whosoever loveth wisdom, let him turn in hither, and he shall find her; and when he hath found her, happy is he if he hold her fast.
