# SOURCE: Antiphons on the Magnificat for Saturday Vespers Throughout the Year.docx, p. 9 (Aspice Domine)
Behold, O Lord, * how the city is become desolate that was full of precious treasure; she sitteth sorrowful that was great among the nations: none can comfort her but only thou, O our God.
