[hymn-lauds]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 316 (The Feast of the Most Holy Trinity)
Tu, Trinitatis Unitas

Thou Trinity in Unity
Who rulest all things mightily,
Bow down to hear the songs of praise
Which we, thy wakeful servants raise.

The day-star's rays are glitt'ring clear,
And tell that day itself is near;
The shadows of the night depart:
Thou, holy Light, illume the heart.

All laud to God the Father be;
All praise, eternal Son, to thee;
All glory, as is ever meet,
To God the Holy Paraclete.
Amen.

[hymn-vespers]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Vespers book, p. 194 (The Feast of the Most Holy Trinity)
O lux beata Trinitas

O Trinity of blessed light,
O Unity of princely might,
The fiery sun now goes his way;
Shed thou within our hearts thy ray.

To thee our morning song of praise,
To thee our evening prayer we raise;
Thy glory suppliant we adore
For ever and for evermore.

All laud to God the Father be;
All praise, eternal Son, to thee;
All glory, as is ever meet,
To God the Holy Paraclete.
Amen.
