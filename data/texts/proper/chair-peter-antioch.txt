[hymn-lauds]
# SOURCE: divinum-officium SanctiM/02-22 [Hymnus Laudes] — check against diurnal
Beate Pastor, Petre, clemens accipe

Peter, blest shepherd, hearken to our cry,
And with a word unloose our guilty chain;
Thou! who hast power to open the gates on high
To men below, and power to shut them fast again.

Praise, blessing, majesty, through endless days,
Be to the Trinity immortal given;
Who in pure unity profoundly sways
Eternally alike all things in earth and heaven.
Amen.

[hymn-vespers]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Vespers book, pp. 290–291, 299–300 (The Chair of St Peter at Antioch)
Quodcumque vinclis super terram

Whatever thou bind'st here on earth in punishment
Likewise remaineth firmly bound in paradise;
What thou dost loosen here by thine authority
Over the heavens ever hath its liberty:
When this world endeth, thou shalt judge eternally.

Glory, O Father, be to thee eternally;
Grace and dominion to the Son adorable;
Unto the Spirit, honour and authority;
Praise everlasting through the ages infinite.
Amen.
