The righteous stand * in the Presence of the Lord, and are not divided the one from the other; they drank of the Lord's Cup, and they are called Friends of God.

[benedictus-antiphon]
# SOURCE: divinum-officium Sancti/06-26.txt [Ant 2] — check against diurnal
These are the holy ones * who for the love of Christ, recked nothing of the threats of men. In the kingdom of heaven the holy martyrs are filled with joy in the company of Angels. O how precious in the sight of the Lord was the death of these His saints who now stand day and night in His presence and are not divided the one from the other.

# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Vespers book, p. 613 (Common of Many Martyrs out of Paschaltide, II Vespers; Gaudent in cælis)
In the heavenly kingdom * rejoice the souls of the Blessed, who follow'd the footsteps of Christ their Master: and since for love of him they poured forth their life-blood, therefore with Christ do they exult for ever.
