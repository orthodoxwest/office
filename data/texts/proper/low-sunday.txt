[benedictus-antiphon]
Thomas, because thou hast seen me, thou hast believed: * blessed are they that have not seen, and yet have believed, alleluia.

[magnificat-antiphon]
After eight days * came the Lord, the doors being shut, and said unto them: Peace be unto you, alleluia, alleluia.

[commemoration-antiphon]
Now when it was late * the same day, the first of the week, and the doors were shut, where the disciples were gathered together, for fear of the Jews, Jesus came and stood in the midst and said to them: Peace be to you, alleluia.

[commemoration-versicle]
V. The Lord is risen from the dead, alleluia.
R. Who for our sakes died and rose again, alleluia.

[collect]
Grant, we beseech Thee, almighty God, that we who have celebrated the Paschal Feast, may, by Thy bounty, retain its fruits in our daily habits and behaviour.
