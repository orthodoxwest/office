O God, who through the preaching of the blessed Apostle Paul hast caused the light of the Gospel to shine gloriously among the Gentiles, we beseech thee that we who keep the feast of his commemoration may feel to our comfort the power of his prayer with thee.

[commemoration-antiphon]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Vespers book, pp. 293, 301 (Chair of Saint Peter at Antioch)
O holy Apostle Paul, * thou preacher of the truth and Doctor of the Gentiles, intercede for us unto God, who hath chosen thee.

[commemoration-versicle]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Vespers book, pp. 293, 301 (Chair of Saint Peter at Antioch)
V. Thou art a chosen vessel, holy Apostle Paul.
R. A preacher of the truth throughout all the world.

[commemoration-collect]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Vespers book, pp. 293, 301 (Chair of Saint Peter at Antioch)
O God, who didst teach a multitude of the Gentiles by the preaching of blessed Paul the Apostle: grant unto us, we beseech thee, that we who honour his Commemoration may perceive the benefits of his intercession before thee.
