[benedictus-antiphon]
This evil and adulterous generation seeketh after a sign; * and there shall no sign be given to it, but the sign of the Prophet Jonas.

[magnificat-antiphon]
As Jonas was three days and three nights in the whale's belly, * so shall the Son of man be three days and three nights in the heart of the earth.

[commemoration-antiphon]
This evil and adulterous generation seeketh after a sign; * and there shall no sign be given to it, but the sign of the Prophet Jonas.

[commemoration-versicle]
V. O Lord, have mercy upon us.
R. For we have sinned against thee.

[collect]
O Lord, we beseech thee mercifully to hear our prayers, and to stretch forth the right hand of thy power against all things that fight against us.
