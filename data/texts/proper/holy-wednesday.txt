[psalm-antiphon]
@use proper/holy-wednesday/psalm-antiphon-1

[psalm-antiphon-1]
Deliver me * from blood, O God, thou God of my salvation: and my tongue shall extol thy justice.

[psalm-antiphon-2]
I received * injuries and terrors from them: but the Lord is with me as a strong warrior.

[psalm-antiphon-3]
But thou, O Lord, * knowest all their plans to put me to death.

[psalm-antiphon-4]
All my enemies have heard of my evil, * they have rejoiced that thou, O Lord, hast done it.

[psalm-antiphon-5]
The Lord helpeth them * to right that suffer wrong, but as for the way of the ungodly, he turneth it upside down.

[benedictus-antiphon]
Simon, do you sleep? * Could you not watch one hour with me?

[magnificat-antiphon]
The handmaid said to Peter: * Surely thou also art one of them; for even thy speech doth discover thee.
