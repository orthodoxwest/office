[psalm-antiphon-1]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds/Vespers books, pp. 531, 469 (Nativity BVM)
The Nativity * of the glorious Virgin Mary, of the seed of Abraham, sprung from the tribe of Juda, of the noble stem of David.

[psalm-antiphon-2]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds/Vespers books, pp. 532, 470 (Nativity BVM)
To-day is the Nativity * of the holy Virgin Mary, whose glorious life illumineth all the churches.

[psalm-antiphon-3]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds/Vespers books, pp. 532, 471 (Nativity BVM)
Sprung from a royal race, * lo, Mary shineth resplendent: by whose prayers we ask devoutly that we may be aided both in mind and spirit.

[psalm-antiphon-4]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 533 (Nativity BVM)
With all our heart and soul, * let us sing glory unto Christ on this holy solemnity of Mary, the exalted Mother of God.

[psalm-antiphon-5]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 535 (Nativity BVM)
With joy and gladness * let us celebrate the Nativity of blessed Mary: that she may intercede for us unto Jesus Christ our Lord.

[psalm-antiphon-4-vespers]
@use proper/nativity-bvm/psalm-antiphon-5

[benedictus-antiphon]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 539 (Nativity BVM)
To-day let us celebrate * with due solemnity the Nativity of God's Mother, the ever Virgin Mary: from whom the Son of the Highest proceeded, alleluia.

[magnificat-antiphon]
# SOURCE: older Sanctoral Vespers (II Vespers, Nativity BVM)
Thy Nativity, * O Virgin Mother of God, hath proclaimed joyful tidings unto all the world: for out of thee hath arisen the Sun of righteousness, even Christ our God: who, taking away the curse, hath bestowed a blessing; and confounding death, hath given unto us life everlasting.

[magnificat-antiphon-first]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Vespers book, p. 475 (I Vespers, Nativity BVM)
Let us celebrate * the worshipful Nativity of the blessed and glorious Virgin Mary: for she hath obtained the dignity of Motherhood, and yet lost not her maiden purity.

[commemoration-antiphon]
This day was born the glorious Virgin Mary, * a child of the seed of Abraham, a daughter of the tribe of Judah, a Princess of the lineage of David.

[commemoration-versicle]
V. This day is the Birthday of the holy Virgin Mary.
R. Whose famous life still sheddeth lustre upon all the Churches.

[collect]
# SOURCE: older Sanctoral Lauds/Vespers (Nativity BVM)
We beseech thee, O Lord, bestow upon us thy servants the gift of thy heavenly grace: that as our salvation began by the Child-bearing of the blessed Virgin, so this devout solemnity of her Nativity may afford us an increase of peace.
