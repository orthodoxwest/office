[psalm-antiphon-1]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 350; Vespers book, pp. 226, 234 (St Andrew)
Hail, precious Cross, * receive the disciple of him who hanged on thee, my Master Christ.

[psalm-antiphon-2]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 350; Vespers book, pp. 227, 235 (St Andrew)
Blessed Andrew * prayed, saying, O Lord, King of eternal glory, sustain me, as I hang upon the tree.

[psalm-antiphon-3]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 351; Vespers book, pp. 228, 236 (St Andrew)
Andrew, the servant of Jesus Christ, * a worthy Apostle of God, was Peter's brother, and partaker of his passion.

[psalm-antiphon-4]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 352 (St Andrew)
Maximilla, beloved of Jesus Christ, * took the Apostle's body: and in a place of honour buried it with sweet-smelling spices.

[psalm-antiphon-5]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 354 (St Andrew)
Thou hast overwhelmed in hell, O Lord, * them that did persecute the righteous: and thou wast his leader on the wood of the Cross.

[psalm-antiphon-4-vespers]
@use proper/st-andrew/psalm-antiphon-5

[magnificat-antiphon]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Vespers book, p. 241 (II Vespers for St Andrew)
When blessed Andrew * came to the place where the Cross had been prepared, he cried out and said: O goodly Cross, so long desired, and now made ready for my eager spirit; fearless and joyful do I come to thee: therefore do thou also receive me gladly, as his disciple, who did hang upon thee.

[commemoration-antiphon]
God bless thee, precious cross, * be welcome to the follower of Him That hung on thee, even my Master Christ.

[collect]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 359; Vespers book, pp. 233, 242 (St Andrew)
We humbly entreat thy Majesty, O Lord: that as the blessed Apostle Andrew stood forth as a preacher and ruler in thy Church, so he may be our constant intercessor before thee.

[benedictus-antiphon]
# SOURCE: draft archdiocesan Lauds book, p. 358 (St Andrew)
Yield up to us * a man so righteous, restore to us a man so holy: destroy not a man so dear to God, righteous, dutiful, and gentle.
