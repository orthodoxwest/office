# SOURCE: divinum-officium Tempora/091-0 [Ant 1] — check against diurnal
When Job heard the words * of the messengers, he suffered it patiently, and said: What! shall we receive good at the hand of the Lord, and shall we not receive evil? In all this did not Job sin with his lips, nor charged God foolishly.
