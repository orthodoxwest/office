# SOURCE: Antiphons on the Magnificat for Saturday Vespers Throughout the Year.docx, p. 6 (Cum audisset Job)
Now when Job had heard * the words of the messengers, he endured with patience, saying, Shall we receive good at the Lord’s hand, and shall we not receive evil also? In all this Job sinned not with his lips, neither charged God foolishly.
