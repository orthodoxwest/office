Push notifications beta

Prefer notifications straight to this device, without a calendar subscription? Enable push and this app will remind you before each hour you selected above. Unlike the calendar feed, this stores your subscription on the server so it can send the alerts.

Changed the hours or times above? Press Enable notifications again to update your saved schedule. On iPhone and iPad, add this app to your Home Screen first — Safari only allows push from an installed app.

Setting it up

iPhone, iPad, Mac (Apple Calendar)

Tap Subscribe above and confirm. Reminders work as configured. If alerts don't appear, check that Remove Alerts is off for the subscription (Settings › Calendar › Accounts).

Google Calendar

Copy the feed URL, then in Google Calendar choose Other calendars › + › From URL and paste it. Google ignores the alerts inside subscribed feeds — after subscribing, open the calendar's settings and add a default Event notification (e.g. 10 minutes) so reminders fire. Google may take up to a day to first sync the feed.

Android without Google Calendar

Install ICSx⁵ (free, F-Droid or Play Store), add the feed URL, and its alerts work as configured.
